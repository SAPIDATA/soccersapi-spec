# Introduction

**Soccer’s API** is a comprehensive and versatile RESTful service that delivers **real-time soccer data** with speed, accuracy, and reliability.  
Built for both businesses and developers — from experienced professionals to newcomers — it provides the easiest way to access and integrate rich soccer information into any application.  

With a primary focus on **accuracy** and **up-to-date coverage**, Soccer’s API unlocks a wide spectrum of soccer-related data, including:

- **Leagues Coverage** – Access nearly **1,000 leagues worldwide**, offering complete insights into the global landscape of soccer.  
- **Live Scores** – Follow matches in real time with instant updates on goals, cards, substitutions, and other key events.  
- **Lineups** – View confirmed starting lineups before kickoff, helping to analyze strategies and player selections.  
- **Statistics** – Explore detailed match, team, and player statistics, from performance metrics to historical records.  
- **Betting Odds** – Retrieve live odds from multiple bookmakers for market analysis and informed betting decisions.  
- **Broadcast TV Listings** – Stay updated with live broadcast schedules to ensure fans never miss a game.  
- **Player Profiles** – Get biographical data, career stats, transfer history, images, and form updates for players.  
- **Detailed Profiles** – Go beyond matches with structured data on leagues, teams, coaches, referees, stadiums, bookmakers, and TV channels.
